Fried Chicken Wings

Below will be a list of ingredients and a step by step method to making these Fried Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 12 small chicken wings
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1.4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • Olive oil for frying

Cooking Method

  1. Step One - season chicken wings lightly with salt
  2. Step Two - Mix together flour, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper and patrika in a wide, shallow bowl. Press wings into flour mixture to coat and arrange onto a large plate with space between. Refrigerate coated wings for 15 to 30 minutes.
  3. Step Three - Dredge wings again in flour mixture and return to the plate. Refrigerate wings once more for 15 to 30 minutes
  4. Step Four - Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 190 degrees C (375 degrees Farenheit)
  5. Step Five - Fry chicken wings in hot oil until crisp and juices run clear, 9 to 12 minutes.
  6. Step Six - Transfer fried wings to a large bowl
