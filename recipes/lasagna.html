Classic Lasagna Recipe

Classic Lasagna

Below will be a list of the ingredients required and the step by step guide to making this Classic Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil, plus a little for the dish
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack of prosciutto
  • tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • a little grated nutmeg
  • 300g pack fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozzarella, torn into thin strips

Cooking method

  1. Step One - To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  2. Step Two - Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack, then stir through the meat mixture
  3. Step Three - Pour over 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season
  4. Step Four - Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.
  5. Step Five - Heat oven to 180C/fan/160C/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm).
  6. Stepy Six - Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made or homemade white sauce.
  7. Step Seven - Repeat until you have 3 layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you can’t see any pasta poking through.
  8. Step Eight - Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top.
  9. Step Nine - Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
